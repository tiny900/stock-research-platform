# Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) — 10-K Annual Report Excerpt (FY 2024)

## Business Overview
Microsoft Corporation develops and supports software, services, devices, and solutions worldwide. The Company operates through three segments: Productivity and Business Processes (Microsoft 365, LinkedIn, Dynamics), Intelligent Cloud (Azure, server products, GitHub, Nuance), and More Personal Computing (Windows, gaming, search advertising, Surface).

## Financial Highlights
- Total revenue: $245.1 billion (FY 2024), up 16% year-over-year
- Intelligent Cloud revenue: $105.4 billion (43% of total), up 22% YoY
- Azure and other cloud services revenue grew 30% YoY
- Gross margin: 69.4%
- Operating income: $109.4 billion (44.6% operating margin)
- Net income: $88.1 billion; Diluted EPS: $11.80
- Operating cash flow: $118.5 billion
- Free cash flow: $74.1 billion

## AI Strategy and Investments
Microsoft has invested significantly in artificial intelligence, including a multi-year, multi-billion-dollar investment in OpenAI. AI capabilities are being integrated across products: Microsoft Copilot in Office 365, GitHub Copilot for developers, and Azure OpenAI Service for enterprises. AI-related revenue has become a material contributor to Intelligent Cloud growth.

## Risk Factors
- Intense competition across all segments (AWS, Google Cloud, Salesforce)
- Execution risk in AI strategy and integration
- Cybersecurity threats and data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- Dependence on continued growth of cloud computing adoption
- Regulatory scrutiny of the Activision Blizzard acquisition ($68.7 billion)
- Talent acquisition and retention in competitive tech labor market

## Capital Return Program
Microsoft returned $34.5 billion to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases in FY 2024. Quarterly dividend of $0.75 per share. The company maintains an investment-grade credit rating (AAA by S&P). Total cash and short-term investments: $75.5 billion.
