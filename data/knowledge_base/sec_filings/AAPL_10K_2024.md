# Apple Inc. (AAPL) — 10-K Annual Report Excerpt (FY 2024)

## Business Overview
Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories worldwide. The Company also offers various related services. Products include iPhone, Mac, iPad, and Wearables, Home and Accessories. Services include advertising, AppleCare, cloud services, digital content, and payment services.

## Financial Highlights
- Total net revenue: $383.3 billion (FY 2024), compared to $383.9 billion (FY 2023)
- Products revenue: $298.1 billion; Services revenue: $85.2 billion
- Services segment grew approximately 9% year-over-year
- Gross margin: 46.2% (up from 44.1% in FY 2023)
- Net income: $93.7 billion; Diluted EPS: $6.08
- Operating cash flow: $110.5 billion
- Capital expenditures: $9.9 billion
- Free cash flow: approximately $100.6 billion

## Risk Factors
- Global and regional economic conditions affecting consumer spending
- Intense competition in smartphone, PC, and services markets
- Dependence on third-party suppliers, particularly for semiconductors
- Supply chain disruptions and geopolitical risks (China manufacturing concentration)
- Foreign currency fluctuations affecting international revenues (~60% of total)
- Regulatory risks including antitrust investigations and App Store policies
- Evolving privacy regulations affecting advertising revenue

## Segment Information
Apple operates in five geographic segments: Americas (43% of revenue), Europe (25%), Greater China (17%), Japan (7%), and Rest of Asia Pacific (8%). iPhone represents approximately 52% of total revenue. The Services segment has the highest gross margin at approximately 74%.

## Capital Return Program
Apple returned over $100 billion to shareholders in FY 2024 through dividends and share repurchases. The company has a quarterly dividend of $0.25 per share. Total share repurchases were approximately $90 billion. The Board authorized an additional $110 billion for share repurchases in May 2024.
