# Alphabet Inc. (GOOGL) — 10-K Annual Report Excerpt (FY 2024)

## Business Overview
Alphabet Inc. is the parent company of Google and several other businesses. Google Services includes Search, YouTube, Android, Chrome, Google Cloud Platform, and hardware products. Google Cloud provides enterprise cloud computing, AI/ML services, and collaboration tools. Other Bets include Waymo (autonomous driving), Verily (life sciences), and various moonshot projects.

## Financial Highlights
- Total revenue: $350.0 billion (FY 2024), up 14% year-over-year
- Google Search & other: $198.1 billion (57% of total)
- YouTube ads: $36.1 billion, up 13% YoY
- Google Cloud: $43.2 billion, up 29% YoY (first full year of profitability)
- Operating income: $112.4 billion (32.1% operating margin)
- Net income: $100.7 billion; Diluted EPS: $8.04
- Operating cash flow: $112.8 billion
- Free cash flow: $72.8 billion

## AI Strategy
Alphabet has made AI central to its strategy through Google DeepMind. Key AI products include Gemini (large language model), AI Overviews in Search, AI-powered features in Google Workspace, and Vertex AI on Google Cloud. The company develops custom AI chips (TPU v5, Trillium) alongside using NVIDIA GPUs. AI is being integrated across all products to improve user experience and create new revenue streams.

## Risk Factors
- Dependence on advertising revenue (approximately 77% of total revenue)
- Regulatory and antitrust challenges globally (DOJ antitrust case, EU Digital Markets Act)
- Competition in AI from Microsoft/OpenAI, Amazon, Meta, and open-source models
- Privacy regulations affecting ad targeting capabilities
- YouTube content moderation and brand safety concerns
- Waymo autonomous vehicle liability and regulatory uncertainty

## Capital Allocation
Alphabet returned $62.2 billion to shareholders through share repurchases in 2024. The company initiated its first-ever dividend ($0.20 per share quarterly) in 2024. Total cash and marketable securities: $100.7 billion. Capital expenditures of $40.0 billion primarily for data center expansion to support AI infrastructure.
