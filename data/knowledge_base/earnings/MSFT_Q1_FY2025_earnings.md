# Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) — Q1 FY2025 Earnings Call Summary

## Key Metrics
- Revenue: $65.6 billion (+16% YoY)
- EPS: $3.30 (+10% YoY)
- Intelligent Cloud revenue: $24.1 billion (+20%)
- Azure revenue growth: 34% (constant currency)
- Microsoft 365 Commercial revenue: +13%

## CEO Satya Nadella Highlights
- AI business is on track to surpass $10 billion annual run rate
- GitHub Copilot adoption accelerating with 1.8 million paid subscribers
- Microsoft Copilot for Microsoft 365 seeing strong enterprise adoption
- Azure AI services usage grew over 100% YoY
- Significant investment in data center capacity to meet AI demand
- Partnership with OpenAI continues to drive product innovation

## CFO Amy Hood Guidance
- Q2 FY2025 revenue expected between $68.1 and $69.1 billion
- Azure growth expected to accelerate in H2 FY2025 as capacity comes online
- Operating margins expected to remain stable despite increased AI capex
- Capital expenditure increasing to support AI infrastructure buildout
- Expects to continue returning capital through dividends and buybacks

## Analyst Q&A Key Points
- AI contribution to Azure growth was approximately 12 percentage points
- Enterprise customers rapidly moving from pilots to production AI deployments
- Microsoft Fabric (data analytics platform) gaining strong traction
- Security remains top spending priority for enterprise customers
- LinkedIn revenue growing driven by AI-powered hiring tools

## Market Reaction
Stock rose approximately +3.5% in after-hours trading. Analysts highlighted Azure growth acceleration and AI monetization progress as key positives. Some concern noted about increasing capex levels and timeline to AI-related margin expansion.
