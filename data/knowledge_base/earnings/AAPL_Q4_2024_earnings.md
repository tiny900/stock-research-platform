# Apple Inc. (AAPL) — Q4 FY2024 Earnings Call Summary

## Key Metrics
- Revenue: $94.9 billion (vs. $89.5 billion year-ago quarter, +6% YoY)
- EPS: $1.64 (vs. $1.46 year-ago, +12% YoY)
- iPhone revenue: $46.2 billion (+6%)
- Services revenue: $24.97 billion (+12%)
- Greater China revenue: $15.0 billion (-0.3%)

## CEO Tim Cook Highlights
- Apple Intelligence features rolling out across iPhone, iPad, and Mac
- iPhone 16 family seeing strong upgrade demand driven by AI capabilities
- Services business reached all-time quarterly record revenue
- Installed base of active devices reached over 2.35 billion globally
- Continued investment in generative AI and machine learning across products

## CFO Luca Maestri / Kevan Parekh Guidance
- Q1 FY2025 revenue expected to grow low-to-mid single digits YoY
- Services revenue expected to continue double-digit growth trajectory
- Gross margin expected in the range of 46% to 47%
- Operating expenses expected between $15.3 and $15.5 billion
- Tax rate expected around 16%

## Analyst Q&A Key Points
- AI features are a meaningful driver of iPhone upgrade cycles
- App Store growth remains strong despite regulatory changes in EU
- India market showing strong double-digit growth as manufacturing expands
- Capital expenditure focus on data center and AI infrastructure
- No specific guidance on Vision Pro sales but "encouraged by developer momentum"

## Market Reaction
Stock moved approximately +1.2% in after-hours trading following the earnings beat on both revenue and EPS. Analysts noted the strength in Services as a positive margin driver and the potential for Apple Intelligence to drive a multi-year iPhone upgrade supercycle.
